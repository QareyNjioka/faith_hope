Activities

Our Activities include

Volunteering

We aim to reach out to the less fortunate in the society. We are called to carry one another's burdens in any way, shape or form.

Charity Work

For every blessing we enjoy we hope to extend the same to those that lack in that area. We are called to share through our testimonials to strengthen others that are going through what we've already gained victory over.

Bible Study

We gather together to share each of our revelation so as to learn from one another and grow in our faith within this family. Join us every Thursday for an encounter with Jesus.
